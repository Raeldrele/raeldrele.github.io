Brand


Recluse Retreats

Tag Line:
Distraction-free to focus on me.

Concept:
Solitude can help us get in touch with ourselves, better focus on our goals, and develop personnally. Recluse Retreats offers solitude and catered isolation. We provide several levels of housing options and guarantee no pesky people interrupting or bothering you. Inspired by Beatriz Flamini's recent 500 day sojourne in a cave.


Housing options:

  • Three-sided cabin: Get in touch with nature by living in it 24/7. These cabins have floors, a roof, and a simple cot. The roof is not guaranteed to be water-proof and with two open walls bugs come and go freely. Remember physical discomfort can focus the mind!
  • Rustic cabin:These cabins come with four solid walls and a water-proof roof. To help you focus, power and running water are not provided
  • Luxury cabin:Our top-of-the-line cabin! Luxury cabins provide power and flush toilets, however power is cut off at sunset and turned back on at 6:00 am.

Amenities: fresh water, vegan food - conveniently delievered to a bear-proof cabinet half a mile away, exercise, fresh air, beautiful stargazing, and more!
